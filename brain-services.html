Brain Services

Tumors

Comprehensive care for various types of brain tumors including glioma, meningioma, acoustic tumor, and pituitary tumor. Our team uses advanced surgical techniques and personalized treatment plans for optimal outcomes.

  • Glioma
  • Meningioma
  • Acoustic Tumor
  • Pituitary Tumor

Head Injury

Specialized treatment for all types of head injuries, ensuring immediate and effective care to minimize damage and promote recovery.

Paediatric Tumor & Hydrocephalus

Expert care for pediatric brain tumors and hydrocephalus, utilizing child-friendly techniques and advanced medical technology for the best outcomes.

Endoscopic Surgeries

Minimally invasive endoscopic surgeries for a variety of brain conditions, reducing recovery time and minimizing complications.

Aneurysms

Comprehensive care for brain aneurysms including clipping, coiling, and AVM (Arteriovenous Malformation) treatments, ensuring precise and effective intervention.

  • Clipping / Coiling
  • AVM (Surgery / SRS)

Epilepsy Surgery

Advanced surgical options for epilepsy patients aimed at reducing seizure frequency and improving quality of life.

Neuro Navigation Guided Surgery

State-of-the-art neuro navigation technology to guide surgeries with high precision, ensuring better outcomes and reduced risks.

Tumour Fluorescence Guided Surgery

Innovative fluorescence-guided surgery for brain tumors to improve the accuracy of tumor removal and preserve healthy brain tissue.
